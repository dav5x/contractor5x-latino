Improved website for Contractor5x targeting local trade companies (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, general contracting, etc.) offering comprehensive AI Marketing services.

**Design Movement:** Industrial Brutalism meets Digital Precision

**Core Principles:**
1. Raw, confident typography that commands authority — like a job site foreman
2. High-contrast black/orange palette evoking construction safety and urgency
3. Asymmetric grid layouts that feel intentional, not accidental
4. Data-forward storytelling: metrics and stats as visual anchors

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Near-black charcoal (#0F0F0F) — conveys seriousness and premium feel
- Primary accent: Contractor orange (#F97316) — safety, energy, action
- Secondary: Warm white (#FAF7F2) — contrast without sterility
- Muted: Steel gray (#3A3A3A) — industrial depth
- Emotional intent: "We mean business. No fluff, just results."

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed sections with hard diagonal cuts (clip-path)
- Left-heavy hero with stat columns on the right
- Tabbed feature sections with large icon numerals
- Staggered card grid for services (not uniform rows)

**Signature Elements:**
1. Bold oversized numerals as section anchors (e.g., "01", "02")
2. Orange horizontal rule dividers that bleed to edge
3. Monospace accent font for stats/metrics

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states that feel mechanical: slight translate-Y, border flash
- Scroll-triggered counter animations for stats
- Sticky nav with transparent-to-solid transition

**Animation:**
- Entrance: slide-in-left for headlines, fade-up for cards (staggered 100ms)
- Stats: count-up animation on viewport entry
- CTA button: pulse glow on idle

**Typography System:**
- Display: Barlow Condensed (900 weight) — bold, industrial
- Body: DM Sans (400/500) — clean, readable
- Accent/Stats: JetBrains Mono — technical precision

## Selected Design: **Industrial Brutalism meets Digital Precision**

**Rationale:** The charcoal + orange palette directly mirrors the Contractor5x brand's existing orange accent while elevating it into a premium, authoritative aesthetic. The bold industrial typography speaks directly to trade professionals who value confidence and results over polish. The asymmetric layouts and data-forward design make the AI Marketing angle feel powerful and credible.
